Your Impact

Thanks to your generosity, we have been able to provide meals, shelter, and education to thousands of people. Here’s what your donation can achieve:

  • ₹500 can provide meals for 10 children for a day.
  • ₹1000 can fund the education of a child for a month.
  • ₹5000 can shelter a homeless family for a month.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How is my donation used? Your donation directly supports the programs and services provided by Hand of Hopes, including feeding the hungry, educating underprivileged children, and providing shelter to the homeless.
  • Will I receive a receipt? Yes, a receipt will be emailed to you after your donation.
  • Can I donate items instead of money? Yes, you can donate books, clothes, and essentials at our donation centers.
